# AtliQ-Hospitality-Analysis-

AtliQ Grands owns multiple five-star hotels across India. They have been in the hospitality industry for the past 20 years. Due to strategic moves from other competitors and ineffective decision-making in management, AtliQ Grands are losing its market share and revenue in the luxury/business hotels category.

This project offers three comprehensive views:

Performance Overview:
Gives a snapshot of vital performance indicators like revenue, RevPAR, and occupancy percentage. Detailed metrics such as average rates and cancellation percetage are included.

Revenue Breakdown:
Delves into revenue distribution across cities, hotel categories, and booking platforms. These insights are crucial for optimizing sales strategies.

Booking Analysis:
Examines booking patterns, cancellations, and guest behavior. This yields actionable insights to improve operational efficiency and drive revenue growth.
